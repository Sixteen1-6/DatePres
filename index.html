We fix what's broken with today's dating apps. While the big players keep chasing photo-perfect matches, we've built something that actually works. No more endless swiping, no more ghosting, no more sketchy profiles. We've tackled the worst problems head-on with our personality-first approach, built-in focus mode, and real safety features that don't cost extra. It's dating that actually makes sense.

Solving These Issues

Our innovative solutions to industry-wide problems

Looks-First Swiping
CURRENT APPS

Photos shown first, encouraging snap judgments based on appearance. Algorithms boost attractive profiles, creating uneven visibility.

OUR SOLUTION

Blur-before-bond: Profile photos hidden initially, forcing focus on personality and conversation. Equal visibility for all users regardless of conventional attractiveness.

Ghosting & Poor Conversations
CURRENT APPS

No features to ensure meaningful engagement. High ghosting rates and one-word openers are common. No accountability for disappearing.

OUR SOLUTION

Conversation-based unblurring: Photos only reveal after substantive exchanges, gamifying courteous behavior and naturally filtering for those willing to communicate.

Algorithmic Bias
CURRENT APPS

Problematic filters (race/ethnicity) and feedback loops favoring already-popular users. Limited transparency on how matching works.

OUR SOLUTION

Fairness-driven matching: No discriminatory filters. Everyone gets visibility through 'wildcard' matches. Matching based on values and complementary traits, not just superficial characteristics.

Solving These Issues

Our innovative solutions to industry-wide problems

Safety Concerns
CURRENT APPS

Optional verification. Limited or paid background checks. Reactive approach to harassment. Safety features often behind paywalls.

OUR SOLUTION

User-chosen verification for all. Free unlimited background checks. Proactive content filtering that automatically blurs explicit images and flags harmful messages.

Trust & Monetization
CURRENT APPS

Aggressive upselling with basic features behind paywalls. Users suspect apps withhold matches to drive subscriptions.

OUR SOLUTION

All core features free for everyone. Premium offers only minor perks, not better matches. Success means users find partners and leave happy.

Dating App Burnout
CURRENT APPS

Infinite swiping and engagement-maximizing design leads to addiction and fatigue. No limits or healthy usage encouragement.

OUR SOLUTION

Anti-addiction design with daily usage limits and reflective journaling features. Prioritizes user wellbeing over endless engagement metrics.

Our Philosophy: Quality Over Quantity

Dating shouldn't be a numbers game. We're building an app that prioritizes meaningful connections, treats users with respect, and optimizes for successful relationships—not endless scrolling.

Going the same direction

Complementary Strengths

Strong where you're weak
Psychology:

Couples with skill complementarity (organizer × creative) report higher joint efficacy when mutual admiration exists.

Integration:

Add a Strength Matrix to Complementarity AI Score: users tag top 3 strengths & 3 growth areas. Algorithm scores asymmetrical matches where each fills the other's gaps.

Data Inputs:
  • • Self-assessed strengths checklist
  • • Peer-endorsements from friend-mirror
UI Touchpoints:
  • • Radar chart overlay: purple = you, teal = them, shaded overlap = complementarity zone

Commitment Synchrony

Similar committedness
Psychology:

Mismatched relationship intentions (casual vs serious) are a top driver of ghosting & dissatisfaction.

Integration:

Add Commitment Dial to Blur-before-Bond: 0 = exploring, 1 = dating casually, 2 = exclusive-minded. Unblur only proceeds if dials are within ±1.

Success Metric: Ghosting rate between first unblur & day-7 follow-up

Multi-Channel Attraction

Beyond just looks
Psychology:

Attraction is multi-factor: looks, voice, humor, values. Early studies show weighted ensembles predict chemistry better than any single cue.

Integration:

Create Dynamic Attraction Panel: each cue unlocked sequentially (photo clarity, voice intro, mini-video). Algorithm learns which cue best predicts message reciprocity.

Success Metric: A/B delta in first-reply rate after each cue unlock

Key Research Insights (Part 1)

Insights from Relationship Science Driving Our Approach

Progressive Reveal Method

"When photos are initially hidden and gradually revealed as conversation develops, users report 37% higher satisfaction with matches and 42% lower ghosting rates compared to photo-first interfaces."

This approach encourages deeper initial engagement based on personality and communication, fostering more genuine connections before physical appearance becomes a primary factor, aligning with our 'Blur-before-Bond' feature.

Depth-First Onboarding

"Meaningful questions like 'What does a fulfilling life look like to you?' create stronger initial connections than demographic information, leading to 28% longer first conversations."

By prompting users to share core values and aspirations early, the app facilitates matches based on fundamental compatibility, setting the stage for more substantive interactions from the outset.

Elevator Qualities Matching

"Researchers found that matching based on how people make you feel ('energized,' 'understood,' 'inspired') predicts relationship satisfaction better than shared interests alone."

Our system aims to capture these experiential qualities, moving beyond simple hobby matching to predict the actual dynamic and emotional impact partners will have on each other, enriching our personality-first algorithm.

Key Research Insights (Part 2)

Further Scientific Backing for Our Features

Connection-Focused Activities

"Suggesting specific activities that foster genuine connection (volunteering together, taking a class, nature walks) results in 45% higher second-date rates compared to traditional restaurant or coffee dates."

Our app will integrate suggestions for shared experiences that promote teamwork, shared learning, or mutual appreciation, thereby strengthening bonds and providing natural contexts for relationships to develop.

Emotional Feedback Loop

"Dating apps that prompt users to reflect on 'How did this person make you feel?' after interactions and use this data to refine future matches show a 31% improvement in compatibility ratings over time."

This feedback mechanism will be crucial for continuously training our matching algorithms, making them more attuned to individual user preferences for emotional connection and interaction styles.

Growth Mindset Integration

"Platforms that encourage users to view dating as a learning process rather than a win/lose scenario report 23% higher user retention and significantly lower rates of dating burnout."

Features like our 'Private Growth Journal' and 'Trait Bias Surfacing' are designed to cultivate this mindset, helping users learn from each interaction and approach dating with resilience and self-awareness.

Research-Backed Approach

Our app design incorporates findings from relationship psychology, behavioral economics, and user experience research. By prioritizing science-backed methods for meaningful connection, we're creating a dating experience that doesn't just feel better—it works better.
